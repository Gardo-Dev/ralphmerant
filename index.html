Ralph Merant

Ralph Merant is a dynamic actor with a diverse portfolio, featuring standout roles in commercials for major brands such as General Mills, Peloton, Delta, and Xfinity. In addition to his commercial success, Ralph starred in the short film The Velvet Kiss and appeared in the play Sealed Orders. His performances are marked by authenticity and versatility, allowing him to connect with audiences across a range of mediums.

Ralph is currently represented by MDT and Aqua Talent Agency in the San Francisco and Los Angeles markets, respectively.
